Privacy Policy

MathAxis - Calculator, Graphing & Converter

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the MathAxis mobile application (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information. The App is published by Tan Ler Yang Justin (“the Developer”), based in Singapore.

MathAxis is a calculator, graphing, and unit/currency conversion tool that runs entirely on your device. The Developer does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. The App has no accounts, no login, and no servers of our own.

1. Information the Developer Collects

None. We do not collect personal data, contacts, location, photos, microphone, or camera data. Your calculations, graphs, equations, and settings are processed only on your device and are never sent to us or anyone else by the App itself.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To make the App work and remember your preferences, the following is saved locally on your device only (in the App’s own storage). It never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Calculation history
  • Theme preference (light/dark) and other settings
  • Saved equations, data tables, and variables in the graphing tool
  • Saved memory value and bundled offline currency rates

You can erase this data at any time by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC. To show ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and process certain information, which is handled by Google - not by the Developer. This may include:

  • Advertising identifier (e.g. Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate, non-precise device and usage information
  • IP address and general device/diagnostic data

This information is used by Google to deliver and measure ads. The App is configured to request non-personalized ads by default. How Google uses data from apps that use its services is described here: Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

You can limit ad personalization in your device settings: Android → Settings → Google → Ads (reset or delete your advertising ID). You may also be shown a consent choice where required by law.

4. Permissions

The App requests the Internet permission. This is used solely to load advertisements through Google AdMob. All calculator, graphing, and conversion features work fully offline and do not require an internet connection.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising is provided by Google subject to its policies.

6. Data Security

Because the Developer does not collect or store your data on any server, there is no central database of user information to breach. Data saved by the App remains within your device’s protected app storage.

7. Your Rights

As we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our side. For data processed by Google for advertising, please refer to Google’s privacy controls linked above. Depending on your location, you may have rights under laws such as Singapore’s Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), the EU GDPR, or the CCPA; requests relating to the App can be sent to the contact below.

8. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party service, which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob / Google Play Services - policies.google.com/privacy
